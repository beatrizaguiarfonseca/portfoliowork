Promoting global education access through digital content, partnerships, and student mentorship.

Overview

The International Study Ambassador Program is a youth-led global education initiative helping students pursue international studies. ISAP has impacted 6.5M+ people with 47k+ followers across social platforms.

I began as an ambassador and grew into VP and Country Manager, overseeing content creation, community strategy, social engagement, and ambassador coordination. Our mission was to provide high-quality educational content in an engaging, youth-driven format.

Social Media & Visuals

I scripted, recorded, and edited reels and TikToks; created SEO-optimized captions; coordinated over 30 volunteers; and designed graphics that explained college applications to students from 10+ countries.

Leadership & Growth

  • Led restructuring of the marketing department and board operations as Country Manager.
  • Delegated tasks to 20+ team members, developed content calendars, and tracked performance analytics.
  • Forged partnerships with Crimson Education, GEDUC, +MAIS, and other major players in education access.
  • Helped grow the Instagram account by 8,000 followers and tripled engagement through strategy & reels.

Reflections

Leading and growing a youth-led organization across time zones taught me not just how to market—but how to lead with empathy, make impact scalable, and speak directly to what our audience needed. This experience shaped my understanding of what makes digital content meaningful and effective.
