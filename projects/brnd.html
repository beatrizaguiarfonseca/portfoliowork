ABDEM — Brazilian High School Debate Association

Led and built communication strategies for Brazil’s largest high school debate tournament.

Skills

Leadership Content Strategy SEO Instagram TikTok Marketing

Overview

ABDEM is a national non-profit organization dedicated to promoting high school debate in Brazil. Through the annual Brazilian High School Debate Tournament, it engages hundreds of students from all 26 states and the Federal District.

As Communications President, I created and executed the digital marketing plan for the 2023 tournament, reaching over 10,000 accounts and engaging 750+ students. I later joined the board to guide future leaders and support ongoing strategy.

Campaign & Communication Highlights

I designed and posted promotional content, managed Instagram & TikTok, and led a team of three to create over 30 pieces of training, guides, and tournament communications.

My Impact

  • Led ABDEM's communications strategy and annual outreach efforts across Brazil.
  • Built brand consistency and voice across posts, email campaigns, and reels.
  • Organized board meetings and collaborated with other presidents on tournament logistics and pedagogy.
  • Continued to support the organization’s leadership as a board member, shaping future communications strategy.

Reflections

Working with ABDEM allowed me to blend my passion for education and communication into meaningful action. I learned to lead a national team remotely, think deeply about how branding influences access, and build sustainable digital outreach from scratch.
