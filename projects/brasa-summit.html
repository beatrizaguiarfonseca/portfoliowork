Overview

As a Global Board Member and Marketing Campaigns Analyst, I led the entire communications strategy for the BRASA Summit Américas 2024—our largest conference yet. From campaign kickoff in May to on-site content during the event in October, I managed both creative direction and technical execution.

How do you turn a student-led conference into an international brand with real influence?

I oversaw social media management, daily post scheduling, real-time analytics, and speaker marketing. During the event, I produced and published live updates, coordinated video/photo coverage, and led speaker logistics and team management on-site.

  • Led strategy and execution of the campaign from May to October 2024
  • Drove a 520% increase in ticket link clicks and 646% increase in reach
  • Produced content daily across Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, and Facebook
  • Generated over 40,000 views and 568% increase in impressions
  • Managed telemarketing efforts that reached 50,000+ contacts
  • Handled on-site speaker relations, team logistics, and content publishing
