Overview

As a social media lead for BRASA Global, I managed the digital presence for the largest network of Brazilian students abroad, with 40k+ followers and over thousands of accounts in monthly reach. I directed strategy, created engaging campaigns, and tracked performance to elevate BRASA’s voice and visibility.

How can we use content to unite 10,000+ Brazilian students in 100+ cities worldwide—and inspire the next generation of global leaders?

I led weekly editorial planning, campaign development, and content production. This included briefs, design supervision, posting, and insights reporting—consistently driving high engagement and brand alignment across platforms.

My Contributions

  • Led and implemented full-scale social media strategy
  • Managed content calendars, captions, visual storytelling
  • Analyzed and reported weekly KPIs to refine performance
  • Collaborated with marketing and creative teams globally
  • Increased reach to 1M+ and followers to 47k+ (IG)
