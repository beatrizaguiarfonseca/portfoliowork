Overview

As part of the Upkey Virtual Internship Program, I joined an international team of students to ideate, prototype, and pitch a technology-based solution to a pressing global issue. We created THERE4YOU — a discreet, smart bracelet built to protect individuals from abuse, violence, and harassment through innovative 4-button safety tech.

“How might we protect vulnerable individuals in dangerous moments while making them feel empowered, not monitored?”

The product and pitch won 1st place out of 700+ global projects in the IE Business School Venture Lab Series and was praised for its vision, feasibility, and emotional resonance. I led the brand identity, UX sketches, and multi-platform marketing concepts.

The Bracelet: 4-Button Safety Technology

Button 1: Turn on/off

Button 2: Emit loud sound to deter threats

Button 3: Alert and notify the nearest police station

Button 4: Send "SOS" to pre-selected emergency contacts
