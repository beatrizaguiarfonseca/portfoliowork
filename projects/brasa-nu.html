Overview

As the Communications Chair for BRASA NU (Brazilian Student Association), I lead the visual identity, social strategy, and content production for one of the most vibrant cultural orgs on campus. From curating bilingual campaigns to organizing professional and cultural events, I help elevate Brazilian voices and foster community for students abroad.

How might we blend creativity and strategy to amplify the Brazilian student presence at Northwestern?

I designed graphics, coordinated event rollouts, created reels, photographed and filmed events, and ensured all materials aligned with both our culture and the Northwestern brand. I also helped plan and promote over 10 events—including panels with McKinsey, fundraisers, and seasonal parties.

My Contributions

  • Designed bilingual graphics and content for Instagram & WhatsApp
  • Captured event photos and edited recap videos
  • Managed social media calendar and audience engagement
  • Co-led event planning, setup, and community outreach
  • Organized campaigns like Valentine’s Brigadeiros & Paint the Rock
