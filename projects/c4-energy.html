Overview

Our team was tasked with evaluating and reimagining the brand strategy of C4 Smart Energy — a clean energy drink aiming to compete with names like Celsius and Alani. This project was conducted in collaboration with Heather Gasper, Vice President of Product Strategy at Nutrabolt, as part of Northwestern's ENTREP 395 course. We led a comprehensive analysis of the market, brand perception, consumer behavior, and go-to-market strategy.

How might we reposition C4 as the go-to clean energy drink for today’s health-conscious, purpose-driven, and hype-sensitive consumer?

Our research spanned competitor analysis, consumer interviews, and visual audits. Ultimately, we presented a brand refresh and campaign activation strategy anchored in functionality, personalization, and movement.

Market Research

We analyzed the growing clean energy market, examined key players like Red Bull, Monster, Celsius, and Alani Nu, and decoded consumer behavior from packaging to performance perception.

Brand Strategy & Repositioning

Inspired by C4’s existing reach and clean formulation, we developed the positioning: “Fueling the Everyday Champion.”

Campaign concepts like #Pop4Purpose and #M4rkableMoments introduced interactivity and emotional connection, aligning the product with routines, rituals, and real people’s goals.

  • Minimalist Can Redesigns for visual clarity & shelf appeal
  • Hashtag campaigns inviting users to write mantras or goals directly on the can
  • Personalization meets performance — redefining what it means to “seize the day”

Final Presentation

Reflections

Working on the C4 brand pushed us to blend consumer empathy with bold brand moves. From understanding the clean energy niche to delivering a market-ready repositioning, the experience sharpened my ability to synthesize research into strategy — and strategy into stories.
